Petra

Petra is an ancient city in southern Jordan, famous for its rock-cut architecture and advanced water management system. Built by the Nabataeans around the 4th century BCE, it is best known for the iconic Treasury (Al-Khazneh) carved directly into pink sandstone cliffs.
